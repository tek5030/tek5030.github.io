3 After experimenting with this segmentation method it should be clear that it is far from perfect.

5 Here are some things you can try in order to improve it:

7 - Try changing the color representation of the image from **RGB** to **Lab**, **HSV** or some other colorspace.

10  Take a look at [cv::cvtColor](https://docs.opencv.org/4.0.1/d8/d01/group__imgproc__color__conversions.html#ga397ae87e1288a81d2363b61574eb8cab).
11

17 can be used to identify the largest connected component in the binary image and remove the smaller ones.
